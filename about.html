About

Learn more about who we are and what we do.

Penn UES is the largest economics organization on campus. We host a variety of economics-related events such as speaker events, coffee chats with professors, academic discussions, and much more.

We have many opportunities for academic and pre-professional life, and foster a great community of students interested in economics. We are a large and diverse society with students from many different majors, and all are welcome to join!

🎓

Academic Growth

Speaker events, professor coffee chats, and academic discussions that deepen your understanding of economics.

💡

Pre-Professional Development

Workshops, networking sessions, and resources to help you navigate careers in economics and business.

🤝

Community

A welcoming, diverse group of students from all majors united by a shared interest in economics.
