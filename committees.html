Our Committees

At Penn UES, we strive to create an engaging community for those with any interest in economics. Get to know our committees and discover the benefits of joining us!

📚

Academic Committee

The Academic Committee of the Undergraduate Economics Society at the University of Pennsylvania is dedicated to engaging students in in-depth discussions about current economic events and trends. Through regular meetings and events, the committee offers insights into the complexities of macroeconomic phenomena, fostering a deeper understanding among its members. It serves as a dynamic platform for students to explore, debate, and gain perspective on the global economic landscape.

🎉

Events Committee

The Events Committee of the Undergraduate Economics Society at the University of Pennsylvania plays a pivotal role in fostering community and engagement among its members. The committee organizes a diverse array of events, including social gatherings and general body meetings to create opportunities for students to connect, collaborate, and share their passion for economics. The committee's efforts are instrumental in ensuring a vibrant and inclusive environment within the society, enhancing both the educational and social experience of its members.

💼

Finance & Outreach Committee

The Finance and Outreach Committee is responsible for some of the most fundamental club operations. Members raise club funds and allocate them accordingly. They are also responsible for connecting the club with other organizations, people, and communities to broaden our reach and bring in incredible individuals that can share insights with us. Overall, the Finance and Outreach Committee enables us to host a myriad of events, from guest speakers, socials, to networking sessions.

📣

Marketing Committee

The Marketing Committee focuses on spreading the word about the society's events and drawing in new members. They use creative strategies to make sure students across campus know about the society and what it offers, and their work is key in growing the society's community and keeping it active as the largest economics club on campus. Key components of the committee include the creation of flyers to spread awareness about speaker events and making new merchandise for the society!

📰

Almanac Committee

The Almanac Committee is in charge of compiling, editing, and publishing UES's Economics Almanac. One of Penn's first economics magazines, the Almanac aims to provide interesting insight to the world of economics: current events, opinion pieces, and more. A new issue comes out once a semester.
