Who We Are

The Penn Undergraduate Economics Society (UES) is the largest economics organization on campus. Our dedicated members enjoy coming together for different economics-related events, community-building activities, and pre-professional workshops. We are proud to bring together passionate people from all over Penn's campus.

Our door is always open to new members, no matter their background or major. Come join us at our next event — we'd love to meet you and share all that UES has to offer!
